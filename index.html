About Tiger POS

Tiger POS is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline the management of multiple retail branches, including stock management, sales processing, user management, and branch administration.

The system offers an intuitive interface, enabling users to efficiently perform daily operations across different branches while maintaining a centralized overview.
